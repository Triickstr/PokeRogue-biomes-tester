Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the PokeRogue Biome Tracker Chrome Extension handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data. All data used by the extension is accessed directly within your browser and is limited to:

  • Reading in-game data from window.gameInfo on pokerogue.net to provide biome tracking features.
  • No data is transmitted to any remote servers or third-party services.

Permissions Usage

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To display the biome tracker widget only when the user is actively interacting with the PokeRogue game tab.
  • Host Permissions (pokerogue.net): To inject scripts into the game page for real-time biome tracking functionality.

Data Storage

No personal data is stored by this extension. All functionality is performed locally within your browser.

Third-Party Access

This extension does not share any data with third parties and does not communicate with any external servers.
